## üéüÔ∏è LOYALTY PROGRAM

### How It Works

The bot keeps track of your visits to the Teddy & Friends play area.

**The rule is simple:**
- Each time you come and spend time in the play area, it counts as one visit
- After **5 visits**, you automatically receive **1 hour of free play** as a voucher!

### What You Can Do:

**1. Check Your Progress**
- Write "loyalty" or "fidelidade" to the bot
- The bot will show how many visits you have out of 5 needed
- You'll see your loyalty card with a beautiful image

**2. Get a Visit Code**
- Before each visit, you can request a special code from the bot
- The bot will send you a 6-digit code (e.g., 123456)
- Show this code to staff when entering
- The code is valid for 10 minutes, so use it right away

**3. Get a Voucher**
- When you reach 5 visits, the bot automatically:
  - Congratulates you
  - Creates a voucher for 1 hour of free play
  - Sends you the voucher code (e.g., TF-123456)
  - Sends a beautiful voucher image that you can save on your phone

**4. Use a Voucher**
- When you want to use your free hour, simply show staff:
  - The voucher code (TF-XXXXXX), or
  - The QR code from the voucher image
- Staff will verify and activate your free hour
- The voucher is valid for 30 days from the date received
